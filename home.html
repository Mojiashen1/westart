Who We Are

We aim to inspire female entrepreneurs to change the world.
We aim to make a case that entrepreneurship augments the literal arts experience.

Startup Weekend

In 30 hours, we teach students to start a startup/nonprofit step-by-step.

Speaker Series

We have invited 30+ alumnae entrepreneurs and VCs back on campus.

Community Brunches

We host bi-monthly brunch meetups among Wellesley, Babson, and Olin entrepreneurs.

Talent Matches

We match top talents with leading tech companies (i.e. Hubspot) and startups (i.e. TechGen).
